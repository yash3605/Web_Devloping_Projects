Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Spotify account?

To create a Spotify account, go to the Spotify website and click on the "Sign Up" button. You can then choose to sign up with your email address or your Facebook account.

How do I reset my password?

If you need to reset your password, go to the Spotify website and click on the "Forgot your password?" link. You can then enter your email address and follow the instructions to reset your password.
